# Auto-select — infographic style

## Visual Style

Choose the most appropriate visual style for the content automatically. Consider the subject matter and pick a style that best communicates the information — clean and corporate for business data, playful for lifestyle topics, technical for scientific content, etc. Use your best judgment to create a visually compelling and informative infographic.
