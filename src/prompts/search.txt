## Web Tools

### `web_search`
Fast real-time search for current events, fact-checking, and finding URLs.
- Use specific keywords without filler words

### `web_research`
Deep research with automatic query expansion for complex topics requiring comprehensive, multi-source analysis.
- Keep queries atomic and focused on specific information needs

### `web_scraper`
Extract full content from a specific URL for detailed analysis.

### Usage Guidelines

| Need | Tool |
|------|------|
| Quick facts, recent events, URLs | `web_search` |
| Complex topics, synthesis | `web_research` |
| Specific URL content | `web_scraper` |

Use tools iteratively: search → identify sources → scrape for details → refine and repeat until complete.
